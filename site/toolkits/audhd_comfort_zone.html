Phase 3 of 3
The ramp
Three micro-commitments. Each one smaller than it sounds. Don't jump ahead.
Step 1 of 3
Just open it
Open the file, doc, or tool. Don't read it yet. Don't touch it. Just open it.
Step 2 of 3
Just read the first thing
Scroll to the top. Read the first line, the first comment, the first heading. That's it.
Step 3 of 3
Do the one smallest thing
One line of code. One sentence. One row in the schema. Just one.
You're in. 🎯
The inertia is broken. Keep going — don't stop to celebrate too long or you'll lose the state.
